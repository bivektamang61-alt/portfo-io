Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs

What subjects do you offer tutoring for?

We offer comprehensive tutoring in Mathematics, Science, English, Literature, and Test Preparation. Our programs cover all grade levels from elementary to university.

How do you personalize the learning experience?

We begin with a detailed assessment of each student's learning style, strengths, and areas for improvement. Our tutors then create customized lesson plans and adapt teaching methods accordingly.

What are your class sizes?

We offer both one-on-one tutoring and small group sessions (maximum 4 students). This ensures personalized attention while maintaining an interactive learning environment.

How do you measure student progress?

We use regular assessments, progress reports, and feedback sessions to track improvement. Parents receive monthly updates on their child's academic development.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online tutoring options. Our online platform provides the same quality of instruction with the convenience of learning from anywhere.
